Interactables

Here are some of my interactable projects. You may also classify them as video games, although some are more complete than others. There will be a link to each, as well as instructions and how to play them.


Foo Foo's Revenge

Or more appropriately titled "Revenge on Foo Foo", you play the part of the field mouse, and it's your turn to bop some bunny heads! There are three platforms to jump on as bunnies rain from the sky at an evermore alarming rate. How many can you bop? (Artwork by Riley Dawson Mullins)

Controls: "A" or left arrow to move left, "D" or right arrow to move right, and "W" or up arrow to jump. You can double jump.


Tanks

Top-down shooter.

Controls: W-A-S-D to control the tanks direction, mouse to aim and click to shoot.


RPG

An open-world style RPG, you control a dude with a sword in a grassland and desert filled with slimes.

Controls: W-A-S-D to move. Click to attack.
