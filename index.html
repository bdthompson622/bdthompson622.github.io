Current project(s)

Projects here are not, per se, completed or finished products, but rather sandbox projects for educational and experimental purposes.
These were created using Unity with scripts written in C#.
To run, download the zip file using the link, extract the files to a folder, and click the .exe file to run.
Select your resolution, and have fun!

  • Revenge on Fufu

    Background art done by Riley Mullins, music written and recorded by myself.
    Use 'a' and 'd' or left and right arrows to move the character (a field mouse) left/right, spacebar to jump.
    Double jump is possible. Survive as long as you can while getting the most bops by jumping on the bunnies!

    Click here to play in your browser!

  • TanksTD

    Use w-a-s-d to move the tank around while aiming with the mouse. Left click to fire the cannon.
    Destroy zombies, avoid soon to be working enemy turrets. No points or objectives, just have fun!
    Zombie will respawn after three hits from the cannonball. Sorry, no sound on this one yet.
    Run as host to start the game, network play is possible, but not properly playable.

    Click here to play in your browser!
